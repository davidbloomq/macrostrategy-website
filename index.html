Macrostrategy Reading Group

Yale University

Macrostrategy, following Nick Bostrom, concerns research aimed at identifying the best possible long-term outcomes for humanity and the actions that improve our odds of achieving them. What trajectories might civilization take over the coming centuries and millennia? Which interventions today would most robustly steer us toward flourishing futures?

This is a multi-disciplinary reading group for discussing works spanning philosophy, economics, astronomy, and other relevant fields. We welcome participants from across Yale interested in rigorous thinking about humanity's long-term prospects.
